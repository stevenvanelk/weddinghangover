This week we had Nathan Holritz on the show! Nathan is the host of the [Bokeh podcast](https://www.photographersedit.com/blog/category/podcast/) and [A Love Portrait podcast](https://www.aloveportrait.com/). He's also the CEO of [Photographers Edit](https://www.photographersedit.com/) and [Miilu](https://www.miilu.com/newsletter/). We talk with Nathan about his transition from wedding photographer to CEO of a wedding photo editing company, about his goals with the Bokeh podcast, about how to feed frozen mice to pet snakes, and how to turn photographers serving jail time into editing machines. Nathan also teaches how to say penis in Japanese, so stick around till the very end.

## Q&A:

- My computer don't click and the pix donut look good. I have sharpie, can I use to fix print?
